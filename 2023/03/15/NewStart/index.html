New Start

2023-03-15 • 分类: 随笔 • 标签: 双语

原文/Orginal

时隔6年，开始更新博客。上次更新我还是个大三学生。

It’s been 6 years since I last updated my blog, at that time I was a 3rd year college student. Now I restart to update.

其实一直不更新是因为我丢失了六年前的所有源文件。

Actually the reason I was not updating is because I lost all the source files from 6 years ago.

一旦要更新就得重新建站，以前写的东西都会消失。

So if I want to update, I have to re-deploy the site and all the content will be gone.

不过也好，以前写的东西现在看来有些许尴尬。

But it doesn’t matter. Well…things I wrote long time ago make me a little bit awkward…

就这样了吧。

That’s it.

更新/Update

我居然找回了所有的md文件！立即更新了网站！然后删除了某些看起来会地铁老人手机脸的post。

I found all the source files! And I instantly put them back, after deleting some weird posts.
